# Molin Site And Marketing Map

This file captures high-level Molin positioning and public-facing site structure.

## Public Site Areas

Molin’s public presence broadly includes:

- homepage
- pricing
- integrations
- customer references
- blog
- documentation
- about/company pages

## Public Narrative

Molin is positioned as an AI copilot for businesses, especially e-commerce stores. The recurring themes are:

- on-brand AI assistance
- support automation
- product discovery and search
- personalized shopping help
- integrations with commerce platforms
- live chat or human handoff
- analytics and operational efficiency

## Product Themes

Use these as the core product buckets in marketing work:

- support
- sales assistance
- product search
- product discovery
- personalization
- integrations
- documentation and training data

## Messaging Guidance

- Emphasize clear business value.
- Keep capability claims concrete.
- Avoid promising unstable commercial details unless verified.
- Avoid competitive or exaggerated claims unless the user explicitly asks for sharp positioning and the claim is verified.
